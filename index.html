The Legend of Chinatown

Founded in 1995, Joe's Shanghai quickly became a culinary landmark in New York City. Famous for our Xiao Long Bao (Soup Dumplings), we bring the authentic, rich taste of Shanghai to the heart of Bowery.

Our dedication to quality and tradition has been recognized by the New York Times and countless locals. Every dumpling is a masterpiece, crafted with passion to share the rich heritage of Chinese cuisine with you.
